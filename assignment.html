About Us

Hey, I’m Nimrod—a backend developer who loves solving problems and building tech that actually makes a difference. I work with Node.js, CodeIgniter, and Firebase, tackling everything from server errors to database configurations. I enjoy figuring out the details, whether it's getting WAMP to cooperate or integrating secure authentication systems. But beyond coding, I care a lot about the ethical side of AI and how technology impacts society. Right now, I’m developing Sauti Salama, a project designed to provide anonymous reporting and emergency support services. My goal is to make sure tech is used responsibly—whether it’s AI-powered content moderation or creating safer ways for people to report sensitive issues. I also like adding a creative touch to my projects. I’ve built interactive webpages with animations, designed birthday sites for friends, and experimented with engaging user interfaces. For me, coding isn’t just about functionality—it’s about making something meaningful, secure, and visually interesting. Always open to new challenges, fresh ideas, and collaborating on projects that push the limits of what tech can do. Let’s build something awesome!
